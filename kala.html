About KalaYatra

KalaYatra is a cultural tourism and handicraft platform that connects travelers with local artisans. We aim to preserve traditional art forms while boosting local economies through authentic travel experiences. Our platform allows tourists to book guided tours, explore handicraft collections, and interact with skilled artisans.

Founded with a vision to celebrate Indian heritage, KalaYatra empowers artisans and helps travelers discover unique cultural stories behind every craft and destination.
